Welcome to RNAseek

RNAseek is a powerful, all-in-one platform designed for transcriptase measurement and comprehensive RNA-seq analysis, featuring an automated pipeline that streamlines every step from raw data to biological insights.

1
Quality control with FastQC and trimming with Trimmomatic
2
Alignment with HISAT2 and BAM generation via SAMtools
3
Quantification with featureCounts for count matrices
4
Differential expression analysis with DESeq2
5
GSEA and KEGG pathway enrichment with gseapy
6
Rich visualization tools for publication-ready figures

With RNAseek, transcriptase measurement and RNA-seq interpretation become fast, reliable, and accessible to researchers at all levels.
